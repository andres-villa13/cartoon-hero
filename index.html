About Slowpoke

He is an amazing pokemon. Despite his silly face, he is a very powerfull water psychic pokemon. It has another form coming from the Galarian region that will lead to different evolutions. Galarian Slowpoke is a poisson psychic pokemon. Here below some cute pictutes of both kinds of Slowpoke

His main powers

  1. Confusion
  2. Water gun
  3. Water pulse
  4. Psychic

Evolutions

Kanto Region (Classic)

In the classic evolution, the outcome will depend on the pokemon Shellder.

Slowking: If Slowpoke is bitten by Shellder in the tail (as usual, since Slowpoke uses it to fish), it will turn into Slowbro. Moreover, during combats and for limited period of time, Slowbro can turn into Mega Slowbro Click here to learn more

Slowking: If Slowpoke is bitten by Shellder in the head (because of the King's rock on his head), it will turn into Slowking. Click here to learn more

Galarian Region

Slowbro: In this region, Shellder bites Slowpoke in the arm. This unleashes a chimical reaction in Slowpoke's body caused also by the species it eat in this region. That is how it gains the venom element wen evolving to Slowbro. Click here to learn more

Slowking: Beucause of the specificities of Slowpoke in this region, when Shelldar bites it in the head, a chimical reaction is produced. That is how Slowking gets the venom type. Nonetheless, Shellder's intelligence increases enough to control Slowking.Click here to learn more
